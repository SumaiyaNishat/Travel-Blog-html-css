Top Destinations

Kuakata, Patuakhali

Kuakata, Bangladesh's "Daughter of the Sea", offers a rare view of both sunrise and sunset over the ocean.The long sandy beach stretches peacefully along the Bay of Bengal, perfect for quiet walks and photography.With its natural beauty and calm atmosphere, Kuakata is a hidden gem for beach lovers and nature seekers.

30 Godown, Barisal

A historic riverside park on the banks of the Kirtankhola River, 30 Godown blends cultural depth, scenic strolls, and vibrant local life.

some of my favorite places in Bangladesh!
